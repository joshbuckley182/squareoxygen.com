About us

We work on personal and commercial projects to enhance living and working spaces with flora. We create interior landscape designs. Our plantscapers source and install plants, and are available to provide all follow up maintenance.

We can also collaborate with your interior design team. Our experts create a selection of plants with the right attributes for your design and for the climate in your space.

We can bring your green ideas and project to life as long as they involve potted plants. We do not work with open ground gardens or plastic plants.
