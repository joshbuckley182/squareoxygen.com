Story

I believe that as with any living thing on the planet, plants deserve a happy live. Watching them suffering or unwell makes me feel sad. Whether this is at a friend's place, or in the bargain bin of a garden centre - I always do my best to rescue a plant.

Perhaps this and my endless love for nature drove me to turn my last four flats into "Eden Projects". I simply enjoy enhancing my habitat with flora and transforming spaces with new arrangements.

Philosophy

A plant should not be a disposable item, and we can help them live happily and decorate your space for years. Watching them grow somehow fascinates, creates a lot of sympathy, and gives a sense of accomplishment. Indoor plants don't just look nice but also improve air quality, mental well-being and reconnect us with nature, which we naturally lack in urban spaces.

I would love to help to bring these benefits to your life. A collection of books, internet resources, and years of experience have helped me to master my passion and become an indoor plant expert.
